History: The Reunification of Britain and Ireland

From the embers of division and decades of distant rule, the Obelisk Compact rose, binding the ancient lands of Britain and Ireland beneath one Crown once more. This union is no mere political maneuver but a sacred reawakening of kinship and shared destiny. The fractured isles have found strength in their unity, their histories intertwined anew—rising like a phoenix from past fires to reclaim their rightful place in the world.

The Compact honors tradition while forging forward, embracing the legacy of our ancestors and preparing for the dawn of a new Empire. Together, these lands shall no longer stand divided, but as one indomitable power, weathering the storms of history and guiding the future with wisdom and might.

Council of Thirteen & Command Structure

The Obelisk Compact is guided by the Council of Thirteen, each entrusted with a vital aspect of governance, war, and restoration. Their combined wisdom ensures no one hand alone holds the weight of the Empire’s rebirth.

Mobile Task Forces

The Obelisk Compact deploys a cadre of specialized Mobile Task Forces to replace the old military order. These units are trained, equipped, and loyal to the Compact’s mission of restoration and dominance.

  • Alpha-1 "Grim Spear": Infantry spearhead, 1,500 troops. Rapid deployment and urban warfare specialists.
  • Echo-12 "Silent Dagger": Espionage and sabotage, 900 operatives. Silent but lethal.
  • Omega-7 "Nightwatch": Airborne reconnaissance and strikes, 800 operatives. Eyes in the sky, claws in the night.
  • Delta-5 "Iron Fangs": Armored assault force, 1,200 troops. Heavy firepower, frontline shock troops.
  • Overseer’s Guard: Elite protective force for O-1, 400 operatives. Unyielding and vigilant.
  • O-5 "Ashveil" Special Task Force: Historical doctrine and unconventional warfare, 600 operatives. Masters of psychological and doctrinal operations.
